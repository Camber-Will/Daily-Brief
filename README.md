# Professional Pricing Calculator

A sophisticated business pricing calculator that calculates true profit based on revenue minus costs.

## Features

- **Revenue Calculation**: Calculate what you charge clients (hours × billing rate)
- **Cost Tracking**: Track both labor costs and non-employee expenses
- **True Profit Calculation**: Profit = Revenue - Total Costs
- **Profit Margin**: Automatically calculates your profit margin percentage
- **Professional Financial Summary**: Clear breakdown for business analysis and client quotes
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Enter Revenue (What You Charge):**
   - **Estimated Hours**: Total hours required for the project
   - **Hourly Rate - Client Billing**: What you charge clients per hour
3. **Enter Costs (What You Pay):**
   - **Employee/Contractor Hourly Cost**: What you pay per hour for labor
   - **Non-Employee Costs**: Click "Add Expense" to add expenses like materials, software, etc.
4. Click **Calculate Quote** to see the results

## Financial Summary

The calculator shows:
- **Revenue**: What you charge the client (hours × billing rate)
- **Labor Costs**: What you pay for labor (hours × cost rate)
- **Non-Employee Costs**: Sum of all expenses
- **Total Costs**: Labor Costs + Non-Employee Costs
- **Profit**: Revenue - Total Costs
- **Profit Margin**: (Profit / Revenue) × 100%
- **Client Quote**: Final amount to present to client

## Example

**Revenue:**
- Hours: 40
- Client Billing Rate: $100/hour
- Revenue: $4,000

**Costs:**
- Employee Cost: $50/hour
- Labor Costs: $2,000
- Non-Employee Costs: Software ($500), Materials ($300) = $800
- Total Costs: $2,800

**Result:**
- Revenue: $4,000
- Total Costs: $2,800
- **Profit: $1,200**
- **Profit Margin: 30%**
- **Client Quote: $4,000**
